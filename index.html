The Biography

My name is Gurleen Singh and am currently a student at Georgian College. I was born in a small village in India. I completed most of my schooling in Akal Academy Reethkheri and completed my last two years in Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur Public School.

I studied at taught at a private programming institute about C++ and Java. I worked as a Samsung employee for 3 months learning about repairing Samsung devices and about sales.

My ultimate goal is to work for a highly reputed company like Google or Microsoft as a developer or any other position in the programming world.
